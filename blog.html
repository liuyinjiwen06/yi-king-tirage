In a Nutshell

Perplexity has launched a Publishers’ Program to share ad revenue with publishers, aiming to compensate publishers for featured content and includes benefits like a free year of their enterprise subscription.

Perplexity has announced the launch of its Publishers' Program, including an advertising revenue-sharing plan as well as offers of a free year of their enterprise subscription with media publishers, which seems like a response to the plagiarism controversy.

This program has enlisted several prominent partners, including Time, Der Spiegel, Fortune, Entrepreneur, The Texas Tribune, and Automattic (WordPress.com). When a user queries in Perplexity’s platform incorporates content from these publishers, a portion of the generated ad revenue will be distributed to them.
